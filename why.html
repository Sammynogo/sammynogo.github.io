Why it Matters!

In a world with ever growing confusion, its essential to have a clear head and to be thinking straight. Teens are soon going grow up and lead our country and we need mentaly stable and good people to take action. Yale medicine says it perfectly when they state, "the brain is going through a highly sensitive period between the ages of 10 and 19, when identities and feelings of self-worth are forming. According to the report, "frequent social media use may be associated with distinct changes in the developing brain," potentially affecting such functions as emotional learning and behavior, impulse control, and emotional regulation."

The world has lived thousands of years without a metal box in our pocket constantly keeping us hooked with instant communication and keeping our attention; We are just now seeing the harms of it.
